My story

My name is Anna, and beauty is both my passion and my calling. For over 5 years, I have helped women feel confident and beautiful during the most important moments of their lives.

I continuously improve my skills and attend masterclasses by leading makeup artists in Europe. My approach is always personal: I create a look that highlights each client's natural beauty.

I specialize in bridal makeup and also create looks for fashion weeks, events, and photo sessions, from soft daytime styles to bold evening glam.
