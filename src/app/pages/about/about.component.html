My story

My name is Anna. I am an international makeup artist with over 10 years of experience in the beauty, fashion, and media industries, collaborating with international teams of stylists, photographers, and production crews.

My portfolio includes work at the Cannes Film Festival, Venice Film Festival, Paris Fashion Week, Miss Universe Germany, as well as events with leading beauty brands such as Charlotte Tilbury, Victoria Beckham Beauty in Berlin.

For more than two years I have also specialized in destination weddings, working with brides across Europe. Together with my team, we provide a luxury bridal experience, including personalized beauty planning, on-location services, and a concierge-style throughout the wedding day, ensuring every detail of the bridal look is flawless from the first preparation to the final moments of the celebration. My philosophy is to create elegant, modern looks that highlight individuality, natural beauty, and personal style while delivering an exceptional level of service to every client.

My team

Professional team of beauty experts working with clients all over the world, we provide complete beauty preparation for the most spectacular events — from flawless makeup to elegant, long-lasting hairstyles. Whether it’s a fashion production, red-carpet appearance, editorial shoot, or private celebration, we work as one coordinated team to ensure every detail looks polished on camera and in real life.

For bridal events, our team offers a luxury, concierge-style experience for destination weddings across Europe. We support you from the first planning steps through the wedding day itself, including timeline coordination, on-location services, touch-ups, and the ability to prepare the bride and additional guests. The result is a calm, seamless process and a bridal look that stays refined and beautiful from morning preparations to the final moments of the celebration.
